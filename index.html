About & Contact

I'm a 24-year-old composer from Austria, working in FL Studio.

I create music for games with a focus on atmosphere, emotion, and gameplay-driven sound.

From intense combat to quiet menus or story moments, I aim to find the sound that fits the experience.

I also produce standalone tracks across different styles.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations or game projects.
